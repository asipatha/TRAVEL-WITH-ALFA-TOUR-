FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking 3–6 months in advance for peak season tours. Some popular treks can fill up 8–12 months ahead.
What fitness level is required? +
It varies by tour. We have options for all fitness levels from leisurely scenic tours to demanding high-altitude treks.
Is travel insurance included? +
Basic travel insurance is included in all our packages. We recommend upgrading to comprehensive coverage for adventure tours.
What happens if a tour is cancelled? +
If we cancel due to safety or unforeseen circumstances, you'll receive a full refund or option to reschedule at no cost.
Can I customize my tour? +
Absolutely! We offer fully customized tours. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements and we'll craft the perfect itinerary.
What's included in the price? +
All tours include accommodation, meals, guide services, and in-destination transport. International flights not included unless specified.

ABOUT ALFA TOUR

WHO WE ARE

MORE THAN A
TRAVEL COMPANY

Founded in 2010, ALFA TOUR began as a small group of passionate adventurers from Sri Lanka who believed that travel should be transformative. What started as weekend treks into the highlands has grown into one of the region's most respected adventure travel companies.

Today, we operate tours across 40+ countries on 6 continents, guiding thousands of travelers each year through the world's most spectacular landscapes. But our core mission remains unchanged: to create authentic, life-changing adventures that connect people with the natural world.

We believe that when you step outside your comfort zone — whether it's reaching a mountain summit or navigating jungle trails — you discover something profound about yourself.
